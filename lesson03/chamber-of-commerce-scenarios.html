Target Audience

The goal of the Irvine Chambers of Commerce website is to inform individuals and businesses about the benefits of the Chamber. The driving factor of this site is to build Chamber membership. The benefits of the Chamber are diverse and often too vast for your needs, generally chambers have various subsets of their membership collected in various ways. There are topical committees that gather like-minded people and companies together. There are newsletters that go to categories of their membership. There are events and content that cater to specific industries or, sometimes even better yet, specific titles. The goal of this site is to provide the necessary information that will help all members and potential members understand the enhanced benefits of full membership.

Scenario 1

The Irvine Chamber of Commerce has a goal to have 35% of the city businesses signed up to utilize the Commerce services.

Scenario 2

The chamber of commerce is an association or network of businesspeople designed to promote and protect the interests of its members.

Scenario 3

Joining the Irvine Chamber of Commerce can help you build your professional network and market your business.

Scenario 4

One of the main purposes of the Chamber is in forming business relationships and partnerships with similar businesses in Irvine

Scenario 5

The Irvine Chamber of Commerce will bring attention through citywide activities to all members.
